Project 1 – Detailed View

lost souls

Inspired by Yoshitomo Nara's exhibition at Museum Frieder Burda in Baden-Baden, these drawings emerged from a place of sorrow and defiance. His delicate yet rebellious child figures resonated with me, echoing the innocent lives lost in Palestine. This work stands as a silent tribute — a meditation on innocence, resistance, grief, and the refusal to look away.
