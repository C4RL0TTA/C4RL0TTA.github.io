A to B

A to B. A logo in the making — shaped by friendship, movement, and meaning. From Aleppo to Berlin, from All to Basic. This project bridges languages and landscapes: Arabic and German, the Middle East and Europe, tradition and design. Together, we are building a brand rooted in fair trade, organic cotton, and the belief that softness, like connection, should travel freely.
